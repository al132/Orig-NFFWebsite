RoadMap



  • Phase 1


    Release first drop of Non Fungible Fighter NFTs on Opensea. As the first drop sells, build a community and release updates on the game.

  • Phase 2


    After the first drop sells, gradually release the other four drops with price increasing. At this point, a custom unique physical standee will be offered to holders (one per NFT).

  • Phase 3


    Once all 10,000 NFT have sold, a rarity calculator will be added to the website. Soon after, the game will be released with each holder owning a unique playable fighter in the game.

About



About the game

We plan to separate our game into multiple different sections so as to add variety and longevity to the project, at first starting simple, then gradually as we gain more and more support we can afford to put more and more time into the game allowing for more advanced options. Our first idea was to emulate the arcade classic Punch Out where your fighter can be used to go through the game, and for NFT holders you can earn some pretty good prizes if you get all the way to the end!

About the line

The Non-Fungible Fighters are a gang of 10,000 unique ruffians on the Etherium blockchain. Minting a Fighter gives exclusive access to all of our upcoming content. In development is a series of minigames built around the Fighters. Whilst the game may be available to non-token holders, those who do own a Fighter will get access to exclusive playstyles and rewards.
